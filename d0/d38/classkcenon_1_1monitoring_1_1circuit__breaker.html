Detailed Description

template<typename T = void>
class kcenon::monitoring::circuit_breaker< T >

Thread-safe circuit breaker implementation.

Implements the circuit breaker pattern to prevent cascading failures. When a service experiences repeated failures, the circuit "opens" and subsequent requests fail fast without attempting the operation.
